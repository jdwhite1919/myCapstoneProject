Fitness in the time of Covid

Covid-19 has disrupted life across the globe. Weddings either postponed or cancelled, restaurants and retail stores changing, people staying home for work: the list can go on and on. Fitness is not different than every other lifestyle changed due to the virus. With gyms either closed or too risky to go to for many people, how do we keep ourselves healthy enough in times of a pandemic?
